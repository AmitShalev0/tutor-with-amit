DiscoverTutor Privacy Notice

Last updated: December 2025

DiscoverTutor collects only the minimum information required to provide login and authentication services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account access.
  • Connect your DiscoverTutor account with LinkedIn (if you choose to link it).
  • Provide core tutoring services, including tutor matching and session management.
  • Improve platform reliability and detect abuse or fraud.

Data Practices

  • All sensitive data is transmitted over encrypted connections.
  • We retain information only for as long as it is needed to operate DiscoverTutor.
  • LinkedIn profile data is stored solely to power tutor verification features and can be removed at any time.

Session, Payment, and Statistics Data

  • Session details (time, duration, status, subject, and participant identifiers) are stored to manage bookings and provide dashboards.
  • Payment-related amounts tied to sessions may be stored to generate receipts and payout summaries; we do not store full payment card numbers.
  • Statistics pages summarize recorded sessions for convenience only and are not financial statements.

Retention and Exports

  • We may retain limited records (for example, payouts, invoices, or security logs) when required by law or for fraud prevention.
  • You may request deletion of your account; personal identifiers are removed, though legally required records may persist.
  • Contact us if you need a copy of your stored personal data or a receipt/invoice re-send.

Your Choices

  • You may unlink LinkedIn or delete uploaded documents from the Tutor Hub dashboard.
  • If you request account deletion, we remove associated personal information from our systems.
  • For questions or requests related to your data, contact us directly.

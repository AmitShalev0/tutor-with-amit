Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you tutor, learn, or do both as a hybrid user, these quick answers explain how the platform keeps everything organized. Still unsure? Reach out and we will walk you through it.

What is a hybrid account?

Hybrid accounts unlock both the tutor hub and the student dashboard with a single login. You can manage your teaching schedule, then flip to the student side to book time with other tutors for your own learning goals.

How do I become a hybrid user?

Open Edit Profile from your dashboard, then pick Become a student (hybrid account). You will complete the same quick form families use for new students. Once you save, the dashboard automatically switches into hybrid mode.

Will families see my private student details?

No. Student records that you create for yourself stay private to admins and the tutoring team. Families only see their own students and the public information on your tutor profile.

Can I edit or remove my student profile later?

Absolutely. Head to Dashboard → My Students to edit notes, update subjects, or archive the record entirely. Archiving keeps your tutor access intact.

How fast are new student submissions reviewed?

Most requests are approved within one business day. If you need help sooner, send a note through the contact page or mention it when booking a session and we will prioritize it.

Who should I message when something looks off?

Use the contact form for anything account related. Include a quick screenshot or student name if possible so we can find the issue faster.

Need more help?

We are happy to hop on a quick call or respond over email. Let us know what you are trying to accomplish and we will point you to the best place in the dashboard.
